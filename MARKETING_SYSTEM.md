# North Fitness Camp: Complete Marketing & Conversion System

This system is designed to convert visitors into paying members through professional messaging, structured follow-ups, and a 30-day engagement strategy.

---

## 1. Chatbot Script (The "Norfit" Persona)
**Objective:** Capture interest and move them to a Free Trial or Enrollment.

*   **Greeting:** "Hi! I'm Norfit. 💪 Welcome to North Fitness Camp—where we're rebranded, reloaded, and ready for your excellence. Looking to start your transformation or just checking our rates today?"
*   **Response to Pricing:** "Our rates are highly competitive for Tarlac's premier 2-floor facility. Currently, our best value is the **30-Session PT Package** which comes with a **FREE 1-Month Membership**. Shall I show you the full breakdown?"
*   **The Conversion Hook:** "We also offer a **Free 1-Day Trial** so you can experience the energy of the camp yourself. Would you like to schedule your trial for tomorrow?"
*   **Closing:** "Kaya natin 'yan! (We can do it!) I'll be here if you need anything else. 🔥"
